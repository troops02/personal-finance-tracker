Background Colors:
Dark Mode Background: Use a deep, muted green or charcoal shade.
Example: #1E2C2F or #212121.
Main Accent Background: A light minty green for sections.
Example: #DFF6E1.

Primary Text Colors:
Dark Text for Light Backgrounds: Use a deep green for text contrast.
Example: #204E4A.
Light Text for Dark Backgrounds: Use an off-white or light gray.
Example: #F4F4F4.

Buttons and Interactive Elements:
Primary Button: A brighter green to draw attention.
Example: #00B384.
Secondary Button/Link Hover State: A slightly darker green for subtle variation.
Example: #007F66.

Highlight Colors:
Icons/Illustrations: Bright but soft complementary colors.
Yellow: #FFC857.
Blue: #6CB4EE.
Red: #FF6F59.

Border and Divider Lines:
A subtle gray for separating sections or inputs.
Example: #D3D3D3.
